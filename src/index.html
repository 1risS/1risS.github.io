irisS

I'm a creative coder and artist based in Buenos Aires, Argentina, exploring the vibrant intersection of art and technology.

My work blends code and creativity through live-coded audiovisual performances, ranging from regular venues to immersive multichannel and fulldome experiences.

I love working with web technologies for their flexibility and power, they let me build anything I could dream up with proprietary software, and more.

Lately, I’ve been diving into physical installations, combining microcontrollers and cutting-edge computer vision to blur the lines between the digital and tangible.
